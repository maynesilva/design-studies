Neo Brutalism - What is it?

It takes inspiration from the raw, architectural Brutalism of the 1950s but adapts it for the digital world. Unlike standard modern web design— which focuses on soft gradients, subtle shadows, and minimalist layouts— Neo-Brutalism embraces the bold and the quirky.

High-Contrast Colors

It leaves pastel tones behind and opts for vibrant, saturated, and sometimes clashing primary colors.

Geometrical Shapes

It uses sharp, basic shapes and asymmetrical layouts rather than smooth, perfectly rounded corners.

Thick Black Outlines

Instead of soft shadows, elements like buttons and cards use stark, thick black borders to stand out.

Raw Typography

It features big, bold, and unpolished fonts (often monospace or heavy sans-serifs) that command attention.
